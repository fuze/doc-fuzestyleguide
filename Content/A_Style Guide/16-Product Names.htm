The following table lists and describes names we use for our products. Consistency is important, so please check this list for guidance any time you need to refer to a product.

Name Definition / Details
Fuze Desktop Fuze Desktop for Windows, or Fuze Desktop for Mac when referring to OS-specific versions.
Fuze Web Our UCaaS web product. Avoid "Fuze for Web".
Fuze Mobile Fuze Mobile for Android, or Fuze Mobile for iOS when referring to OS-specific versions.
Assistant Fuze mascot and helper bot for all apps.
Person or user Users of our products. Avoid customer or colleague.
Voice Overarching category (as termed by Marketing) that references the calls portion of our apps and services.
Also sometimes referred to as Softphone.
Call Action and functionality available via Fuze Voice.
Video Overarching category (as termed by Marketing) used to reference the meetings part of our apps.
Meeting Primary action performed in the Video portion of our apps.
Also referred to as Fuze Meeting and inclusive of collaboration.
Collaborate An inherent part of Meetings. Interactive actions like share screen, share apps, content, and whiteboard
(not to be confused with our collaboration SKU).
Messaging Overarching category (as defined by Marketing) for the chat portion of our apps.
Chat Action you perform in Messaging; sub-part of a conversation.
Conversation Represents the broader correspondence between users and groups beyond just chatting.
A conversation is inclusive of call and meeting history, shared content, and chat correspondence.
Desk Phone Preceded by the name of the manufacturer if specifying ([Manufacturer Name] Desk Phone).
Headset Preceded by the name of the manufacturer if specifying ([Manufacturer Name] Headset).
Fuze for Chrome Click-to-call and meetings extension for Google Chrome™, and Google Calendar™.
Fuze for Edge Click-to-call Microsoft™ Edge extension.
Fuze for Firefox Click-to-call Mozilla® Firefox® extension.
Fuze for Outlook Integration for creating meetings via Microsoft Outlook™.
Fuze for Salesforce Integration for Salesforce CRM.
Fuze Connect At-a-glance CRM or Helpdesk data for calls.
Fuze Rooms Cloud-based software for Windows and macOS to connect Fuze apps with conference room hardware.
Telepresence Connect Fuze product for conference rooms with non-video, voice-only units.
Fuze Hub Central administrative hub for locations, user, and device management.
Fuze Data Real-time views of call data for departments and users.
Fuze Status Live information on system and service status.
Fuze Portal Advanced configuration and call flow management.
Fuze Community Administrator community and help center.
